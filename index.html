About me

I am a senior reserch scholar in Computational Structural Biology Lab at the Department of Bioscience and Biotechnology, Institute of Technology Kharagpur , India. My current research work focus on conformational analysis of protein-RNA recogniton in terms of protein side-chain rotamers and RNA conformers. My research involves the curation of data set of protein-RNA complexes from RCSB PDB of the protein-RNA complexes. For some of my reserch works requires development of webservers and its hosting to our local server. Moreover, I have been working Molecular Dynamics Simulation of biological macromolecules using GROMACS MD Simulation software.
